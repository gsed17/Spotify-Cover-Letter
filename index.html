Cover Letter — Spotify

Dear Spotify Hiring Team,

I have been following Spotify's careers page closely, and when I saw the Product Manager – Music Expression role, I knew it was the opportunity I have been working toward.

I am an aspiring product manager with a deep obsession with the music industry — specifically what makes artists succeed and why certain moments in music culture resonate when others don't. That curiosity is what drove me to pursue a degree in Business Analytics and Information Systems, because I believe the future of music is built at the intersection of data and human experience. Spotify sits exactly at that intersection, and this team sits at the heart of it.

At ConnectWise, I worked directly with engineering teams to identify gaps, write user stories, and drive features from problem to launch. I learned how to move fast, stay aligned across functions, and keep execution on track when priorities shifted — which I understand is central to this role. At Heritage Insurance, I built the analytical foundation to work with large, complex datasets and translate them into decisions that mattered. Neither company was in music, but every skill I built was in service of getting here.

What excites me most about this role is the Music Context portfolio — Song DNA, About the Song, Countdown Pages. These are products that give artists a voice beyond the music itself, and I think that storytelling layer is where the next generation of fan connection gets built. I want to be part of building it.

I would love the opportunity to bring my product instincts, analytical background, and genuine passion for the music industry to your team.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,
Garron Seddon
